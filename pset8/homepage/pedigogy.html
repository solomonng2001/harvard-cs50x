Pedigogy"

How best to learn and memorise the multiplication table? Follow the following advice and learn better! Remember work smart not work hard!

Active Recall is a learning strategy utilises pratice testing to maintain what has been learnt. In contrast to passively reviewing content repeatedly, active recall strengthens memory efficiently. "Test Myself" therefore helps you utilise the powerful tool of active recall.

Spaced Repetition is about spreading out learning or practive over time, in contrast to mass memorisation or practice in quick succession. Therefore, learn, practice and review every couple of days to distribute learning and better retain memory.
