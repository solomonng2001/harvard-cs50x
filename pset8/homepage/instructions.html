Instructions for "Test Myself"

Unsure? Clueless? Fret not! Follow the following simple instructions and continue learning! Remember practice makes perfect!

Respond to the question and enter your answer in the input box provided. Then, click on "Check Answer" to check the accuracy of your answer. Click on "Next Question" to proceed to the next question.
If answer is wrong, input box will be coloured red and "Wrong!" will appear.
If answer is correct, input box will be coloured green and "Correct!" will appear.
Click on "Learn From My Mistakes", to see dropdown list of mistakes made and the correct answer. Suppose the question was "What is the value of 2 X 5" and the wrong answer "1" was entered, when the correct answer should have been "10", "2 X 5 = 10, Not 1" will appear under the dropdown list.
